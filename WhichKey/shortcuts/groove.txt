CTRL+P
Play or pause

CTRL+F
Skip to next song

CTRL+B
Restart current song or skip to previous

F9
Volume up

F8
Volume down

F7
Mute volume

CTRL+ENTER
Select an item and enter selection mode

CTRL+A
Select all

DEL
Delete selected item(s)

CTRL+SHIFT+P
Play selected item

CTRL+T
Repeat on or off

CTRL+H
Shuffle on or off

CTRL+Q
Search
